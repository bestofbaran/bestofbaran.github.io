About the club

The club Best of Baran was started in December 2010 as a Facebook group by 17 year old Ashish Agarwal. Intention of this group was to make it easy to communicate, advertise, promote and spread awareness among Baran's people. Within a year the group became popular among Facebook users of the city and hence the first online club of Baran was established.

Later in April 2012, the club organized it's first online event B'day of Baran which was a huge success with a decent number of participant. There were three competitions conducted in this event among which Mr. and Miss Baran got the highest amount of attention.

Today the club has connected than 3700 members and serving its purpose by providing several services that will be helpful in bringing a revolution in the city.
